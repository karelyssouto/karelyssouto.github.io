    Learn a little bit about me

    I’m a Full-Stack web developer and a visual artist. With a background in dance and Hospitality, I’ve learned how to complete tasks under tight deadlines as well as paying attention to all the details that make up a project; both user-facing and backend management.
    Since I was young, commanding visual techniques to communicate compelling ideas has been a passion. Inspired by the distinct cultures of my upbringing: Venezuelan, Spanish and American; I strive to innovate how we as a people communicate ideas by blending modes of communication not only across industries but also across cultures. Growing up as a dancer taught me innovative and creative ways to share my ideas without the need of words, and all of these principles I follow whenever I start a project.
