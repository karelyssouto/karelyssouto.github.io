WIT ReDesign Competition.

Web Design

Client: Women In Technology

"For over 25 years, Women in Technology (WIT) has worked to empower and educate women and girls about opportunities in science, technology, engineering, arts, and math (STEAM) by educating middle and high school girls, and young women in college, and by sharing the stories of successful professional women in STEAM." - WIT WEBSITE

Women In Technology has organized a competition to redesign their current website and the challenge was to create a user-friendly, easy to navigate design for the WIT website that appeals to girls and women, creates an engaging experience to keep visitors coming back, maintains consistency and accessibility on any device, and showcases the dynamic brand and impact of WIT

This challenge provided me the opportunity to work in a team with two other designers and through that experience I've learned how to create intituitive UI and to create an engaging website that will help this organization mantain engagement with their users.
